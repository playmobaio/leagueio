How To Play

Objective: Survive for as long as possible.

Controls: Right click on your mouse to navigate to a point on the screen. Q, W, E to use an ability.

Projectiles: On impact projectiles will reduce your health. Projectiles may affect an area or may fly across the screen. Outlined projectiles have not taken effect.

Abilities: Use Q to teleport a short distance. Use W to temporarily increase player speed. Once a player has cast an ability there is a delay before the ability effect is realized. Once the ability is cast there is a cooldown before the player may cast again.
